November 3 & 4, 2023

About Zone Flim Festival

Zone Productions wants to organize a vibrant event that will bring together a diverse gathering of individuals from the film, Music and Visual Art community.

Zone Productions will host a two-day festival on November 3-4, 2023, at Trilith Town Stage in Fayetteville, GA. Featuring visual art, film, music, and comedy, the event aims to unite creatives, encourage collaboration, and celebrate diverse artistic expression against the backdrop of Trilith Studios. Save the date for a vibrant cultural celebration.
